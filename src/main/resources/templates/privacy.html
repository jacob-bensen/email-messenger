Privacy Policy

This is a placeholder privacy policy. A full privacy policy drafted by legal counsel will replace this page before MailIM accepts payments or EU/CCPA-covered users. We recommend using a generator such as Termly or Iubenda in the interim.

Overview

MailIM ("we", "our", or "us") is a SaaS application that transforms email inboxes into a chat-style conversation view. This page describes how we handle your data.

What we collect

  • Email content — We read your emails via IMAP to display them in MailIM. Email data is stored in our database to power the conversation view.
  • Account information — Email address and authentication credentials when you sign up.
  • Usage data — Basic analytics to improve the product (page views, feature usage).

How we use your data

  • To display your email threads as conversations in MailIM.
  • To send you transactional emails (account notices, reply confirmations).
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising.

Data storage

Data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on our cloud infrastructure. IMAP credentials are stored encrypted at rest.

Your rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing support@mailaim.app.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email support@mailaim.app.
