Frequently asked questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes — upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime from your account settings. Annual subscribers get a prorated refund on downgrade.

Is there a free trial on paid plans?

Personal and Team plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card is required to start the trial.

Are there any limits right now?

No. For a limited time every account has full access — all features and no mailbox, thread, or search caps. The paid plans above show how pricing will be structured later; we'll give plenty of notice before anything changes.

How is my email data handled?

Email content is stored only to render your conversation view — we never read it for advertising or sell it to third parties. Full details, including data retention and deletion, are in our privacy policy (coming with launch).
