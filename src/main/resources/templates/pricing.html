Frequently asked

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime from your account settings. Annual subscribers can change tier mid-cycle and we’ll prorate the difference.

Do I need a credit card to try MailIM?

No. The Free plan is free forever and the 14-day Personal trial requires no card. Add one only when you’re ready to keep going.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your account and threads stay accessible on the Free plan. Upgrade anytime to restore full features. Hard delete is one click in Settings.

Does MailIM work with Gmail and Outlook?

Yes. Any IMAP/SMTP mailbox is supported — Gmail, Outlook, iCloud, Fastmail, and self-hosted servers.

Is my email private?

Your messages stay in your account. We never sell, share, or train models on your email content.
