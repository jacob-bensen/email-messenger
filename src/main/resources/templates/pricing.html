Frequently asked questions

What's the difference between Free and Pro?

Free gives you every feature with up to 3 mailboxes and your most recent 500 threads. Pro lifts that to 5 mailboxes and your full, unlimited thread history for $5/mo.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes — upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime from your account settings. Annual subscribers get a prorated refund on downgrade.

Is there a free trial on Pro?

Yes — Pro includes a 14-day free trial. No credit card is required to start it, and you can stay on Free as long as you like.

What's a Business plan?

Business is for teams and organizations that need group rates, higher mailbox limits, or custom terms. Pricing is tailored to your needs — get in touch and we'll put together a quote.

How is my email data handled?

Email content is stored only to render your conversation view — we never read it for advertising or sell it to third parties. Full details, including data retention and deletion, are in our privacy policy (coming with launch).
