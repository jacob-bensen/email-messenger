Common questions

Do I need a credit card to start?

No. The Free plan requires no payment info. Paid trials also start without a card.

Which email providers work?

Any IMAP/SMTP mailbox — Gmail, Outlook, Fastmail, iCloud, ProtonMail Bridge, or self-hosted.

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

Is my email data safe?

MailIM reads your emails to display them as conversations. We never sell or share your data. See our Privacy Policy.
