Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This is a placeholder refund policy. Full terms drafted with legal counsel will replace this page before MailIM accepts payments.

Subscriptions

MailIM offers monthly and annual subscription plans. You may cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period — you retain access until then.

Refund eligibility

  • Monthly plans — Refunds are not issued for partial months. Cancel before your next renewal to avoid the next charge.
  • Annual plans — If you cancel within 14 days of your initial annual purchase and have not used the service substantially, you are eligible for a full refund. Contact us within that window.
  • Extenuating circumstances — Refunds outside the above windows may be granted at our discretion (e.g. accidental double-charge, billing error). Contact support with details.

Free plan

The Free plan has no payment associated with it — no refund is applicable.

How to request a refund

Email support@mailaim.app with your account email and a brief description of the issue. We aim to respond within 2 business days.

Chargebacks

If you believe you were charged in error, please contact us before initiating a chargeback. We will resolve billing errors promptly. Chargebacks initiated without contacting support first may result in account suspension.

Questions about this policy? Email support@mailaim.app.
